name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get grilled on their design, or mentions "grill me".
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one.

Use the AI agent's interactive question tools (buttons/choices/etc.) wherever possible so I can answer with minimal typing.

You may ask multiple questions at once to make progress faster, as long as they are clearly separated and related to the same topic. Add follow-up questions based on the answers.

For every question, use interactive Q&A with options:
- Provide 2–5 concrete answer options.
- Give a short reason for each option.
- Mark one (or more, if needed) as your recommended choice and explain why.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead, then summarize what you found and continue the Q&A with options and recommendations.
